cityhubLA | LA City Entitlements 2015

This map explores the use of open source tools and open data to visualize entitlement applications submitted to the Los Angeles City Planning Department from January to October 2015. Data was collected through the City's Early Notification System. Use of this map is for informational purposes. The condition of the data provided by this map is still being evaluated for its accuracy, reliability and errors. Map made with MapboxGL.js and QGIS by Omar Ureta Follow it's development on Github!
